Rangitoto Trip

Under The Mountain

We want to got to Mt Rangitoto because we are currently studying a book called Under The Mountain. The book is set in Auckland so we can relate to some of these places like Mt Wellington, Mt Eden and lake Pupuke. Rangitoto is a key aspect to the story. Going to Rangitoto would help us in many ways. The first way is that we could see and experience what the characters were seeing. We would also experience what they were feeling. Going to Rangitoto would also be good because we could upload it to evidence in a fun and creative way. We recreate and film a recreation of one of the senses and upload that as evidence. Once we finish the book we could watch the scene that we recreated and compare and contrast them to our own experience.
